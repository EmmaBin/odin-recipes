Chinese Roast Duck

People cook duck in different type of dishes around the world, but the most popular way to cook duck is roasting.

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • hoisin sauce
  • dark soy sauce
  • water
  • garlic
  • cloves grind
  • ginger
  • five spice
  • duck

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, add marinate sauce: soy sauce ½ cup, hoisin sauce ½ cup, dark soy sauce 2 tablespoons, water 1 cup, garlic 2 cloves grind, ginger 3 slices grind, anise 3, clove 3, and five spices ⅛ teaspoon. Mix them well.
  2. Then, wash and clean the duck very well, remove all the guts and organs.
  3. After that, put the whole duck into the marinated sauce for few hours (the best result is overnight). Try to flip the other side after a few hours.
  4. Preheated the oven at 375 F.
  5. Wrap the wings with foil to prevent the wings get burned. Next, bake the duck at 375 F for an hour. After that, remove the foil and change the temperature to 425 F and bake 15-20 minutes until it reaches 165 internal temperature and nice golden brown.
  6. Rest for 10 minutes and chop it into pieces.
